CORRECT LEADS

Helping you stop chasing leads and start closing deals

First, you must realize that consumers have the ultimate power, the power of choice. Correct Leads is therefore determined to help you stop wasting time by providing verified leads that result in real sales. Finding leads can be a complex and monotonous process that becomes simple and easy with our database.

By using Correct Leads services, you will have access to our cloud database that connects you directly with potential clients, leaving you with more time to focus on generating sales. Our success is directly linked to your success.

Concurring Your Market

Over 54% of businesses state that their greatest challenge in generating leads is a lack of resources in staff, budget, or time, following by the lack of high quality data lists that will drive a successful campaigns. We are here to change all that. With verified potential clients, you can stop worrying about marketing reach and focus again on the authenticity, design, and mission of your campaign. We will bring the customers; you just need to close the deal.

Achieving Your Goals

With our help we will make sure you:
  • Save time and effort
  • Grow your business
  • Focus on real potential clients
  • Build connections that result in more sales

Why Choose Correct Leads?

As one of the newest lead generating databases on the market we are determined to push the boundaries of technology.
  • Our database provides 99.9% verified leads, including business and social information for potential clients.
  • We have a lower cost per-lead than any other competitor.
  • 24/7 customer support makes sure that you are constantly striving forward.

How We Do It

We utilize eGrabber, the industry’s most accurate contact findings and appending research technology. Additionally, we hold a number of patents in the field of expert searching, fuzzy matching and intelligent parsing of Internet data. This gives you a competitive advantage, as we are able to make sure to avoid duplicate leads or ghosts, and instead focus on real people. Out database also contains the business and social information of companies and contacts.

Features

99.9% Verified Leads eGrabber has been working in search engine and data mining since 1996. They have developed their proprietary technology that significantly improves the process of researching for real leads. We will provide you with 99.9% verified leads to ensure that you get the highest conversion rates possible. Client support Our team is available around the clock through phone, emails, and live chat to help you find the best leads possible. The team is quick to respond to any problems you might be facing with our platform and ensure that it gets resolved at the earliest so that their workflow is not affected. Download large data When you select a subscription package, you are then authorized to download a certain number of records. The number of records you can download each month depends on the package you have subscribed to. The downloaded content can be saved in Excel file format for easy use. Ad-hoc feature The Ad-Hoc Feature is available with our premium package. It allows you to download and exceed the number of records allotted to your account for an additional fee. You can choose to opt-in to this feature at the time you exceed your record limit.

Subscriptions

Our varying packages make sure that your business receives the level of care and support that is appropriate for you needs. We allow you to upgrade or stop membership at any time. As for pricing, we have the lowest rate per-lead in the business, while still requiring a standard of 99.9% verified potential leads within our database.
